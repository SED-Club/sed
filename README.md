# 🌐 SED(Science, Envrionment, Development) Website

Welcome to the official repository of SED Science Club! 🪐 SED Science Club an international science club connecting passionate high school students worldwide who love Science (Biology, Chemistry, Computer Science, Environmental Science, Physics/Astronomy, Psychology/Neuroscience).

## 🔭 About SED Science Club
* 🎯 Our Mission: To develop the knowledge and passion in science
* 👥 Target Audience: Mainly Middle/High School Students around the World
* 🧪 What Do Club Members Do: Each Club is assigned for the specific team based on their interests after acceptance to the club based on their response to application form.

## ✨ About Website/ Detail About Club's Activity

* 🏠 Home: Introduction to our club's goals, vision, and core values. The Activities/Project, Rule, etc. are also introduced
* 📚 Magazine Team: A showcase of our past issues. Curiosite is a monthly issued magazine made by SED Science Club's Magazine Team.
* 💬 Research Team: Members of Research Team is Passionate about finding new ideas and dive into their interests, specifically in science.
* 📡 SpreadingIdea Team(SI Team): SI Team is a team that makes a Tutoring Videos about science, as well as study guide and worksheet.
* 📝 Appplication: Check if Application is available, and become a member of SED Science Club.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack
* 🎨 Frontend: HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript
