Welcome to SED SI Team Website!

Introduction



SED Spreading-Idea(SED SI Team) is a newly built team to spread scientific information and education around the world, including making tutoring videos or competition questions.

Values/Goal


1. Passions


In SED SI Team, each member is assigned to their own field that they are most passionate about. SED SI Team members are expected to be committed to their passion.


2. Development


By making tutoring videos, study guides, and etc., SED SI Team members aim to help other students around the world to learn and gain better understanding of various concepts. In addition, development does not only mean helping others to learn, but also helping SED SI Team members develop their own knowledge and skills.
