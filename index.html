About SED.....

SED, an international science club mainly for middle/high school students and founded by Idam Suh, stands for Science, Environment, Developent.

"S" represents the club's passion in science, and E, stands for environment, symbolize the importance of the sustainability where we can't proceed with scientific technological development just pursueing human's convenience, but only pursue no-damage-to-nature development. Lastly, "D" represents the development of passion/interest of members specifically in science. These components also well signifies the core value of the club.

The club deals with 6 scientific fields/subjects in total. 6 subjects are listed below


- Biology

- Chemistry

- Computer Science

- Environmental Science

- Physics/Astronomy

- Psychology/Neuroscience

What do SED Club Members do?

SED Club is consisted with two team: Resarch Team, and Magazine Team.

1. Research Team

Research team member conduct research together to create positive externality out of scientific findings. The Team is consisted with 2 division. The middle/high school students from Asia, Europe, Oceania, or Africa can join Research Team Divison 1 and students from American continent are able to Research Team Division 2.

2. Magazine Team

Magazine Team members writes at least one article per person for monthly science Magazine named "Curiosite". More information is available in Magazine section.

Our Values/Rules in the Club

There are some values that SED pursues in addition to 3 core values(Science, Environment, and Development). This is also some requirement for eligibility of SED Application Form.

1. Academic Honesty

SED members are not permitted to use AI and Plagiarism. If members use external sources, they should cite the work.

2. Responsibility/Passion

While SED don't require the perfect performance or specific talent in science, members should be responsible to their tasks/roles in the club and should be passionate about science.

3. Respect/ Non-violence

SED Members should be respectful to each other and there should be no violence in SED; this includes disrespectful/violent/bad words during the communication.
